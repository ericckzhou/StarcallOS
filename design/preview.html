Design intent Each card is a star. Status is the dot color (cool blue = understood, amber = learning, red-shift = needs review, dim grey = unseen). The called star wears the cherry-blossom halo — Study Next has chosen it for you. Trajectory and evidence-strength sit in the small mono-font row at the bottom, dense but non-shouting.

Zoom discipline Three discrete tiers, not infinite-zoom exploration (anti-goal preserved). At galaxy scale, edges are dotted and only the brightest stars are labeled — you see structure, not detail. At constellation scale, edges become solid with relationship labels (prereq, used in, example of). At star scale, the concept owns the canvas with orbiting attempt-history satellites — this is the Concept Detail entry point.
